My Favorite Games


This is juat a list of some of my favorite video games (as of this time), and why I enjoy them.

  • Hades

    • I have always had an interest in Greek mythology, especially more obscure myths. However, it also is mainly a story revolving around family and the complex aspects of personal connections and relationships. It also has a stellar soundtrack and satisfying roguelike gameplay.


  • Mariokart 8 DELUXE

    • The longest running game in the series, along with the largest roster of tracks and characters, constant replayability and essential gimmicks makes it one of my favorite entries in the series.


  • Mario & Luigi: Bowser's Inside Story (2009)

    • One of the first ever games I played during my childhood. Even without the context of the first two games, I always found myself being drawn back by the humor, characters, environments and artstyle along with being my favorite implementation of turn-based combat.


  • Mario & Luigi: Brothership

    • As the most recent installment of the franchise I found it an enjoyable experience as a new-ish era of what is hopefully a true revival of the series. I found myself being genuinely drawn in by the characters and their relationships along with some wonderful character designs and a decent soundtrack.


  • Animal Crossing: New Horizons

    • Plain and simple chill fun, great for casual enjoyment. The fact that the whole series includes exclusive content for each season is what truly makes the game have its draw over the years.


  • Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

    • A wide, expansive world where you can go almost anywhere. The combat is fun, the game encourages unorthodox solutions to almost every problem and there is truly no one way to play. The entire world itself is beautiful, and when it wants to unnerve or or frighten the player, it excels.


  • Minecraft

No matter how much time I take away from the game, I will always come back just to experience my greatest memories from my childhood.
